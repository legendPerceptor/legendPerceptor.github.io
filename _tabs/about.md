My interests lie in several fields. If you found common interesting points, I'd love to create some nice pieces together with you.

**Music**: singing, music composing, piano playing. I regularly pratice singing and piano playing. They bring a lot of joy and feeling of existence for me.

**Videography**: filmmaking, video editing. Video editing takes a ton of time. I currently have a full-time job, which diminishes the time I have to do video editing. But I am still very interested in this field.

**Digital Painting**: I paint with a Wacom tablet and Photoshop. I am trying to learn from WLOP and some great digital painters to create my own gallery of paintings.

**Photography**: I've been doing photography regularly since my second year in the college in 2018, and did a ton of commercial photography before I joined a tech company to have a full time job. In my spare time, I will still take photos purely for artistic beauty. I don't regularly post to the public as I use WeChat for my mainstream photo posting and commercial photos are delivered privately to the clients. You can check out [Yuanjian's Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/yuanjianliu/) to see part of my work.

**Aviation**: I trained for 4 years in the spare time to fly a single-engine land type aircraft Cessna 172M, and possess a private pilot license.
